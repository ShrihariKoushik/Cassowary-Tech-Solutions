White Paper

Cassowary Tech Solutions

Domain Training & Project Mentorship for Engineering & Technology Students

Version 1.0  ·  2026 Confidential — For Student Use Only
📋

1. Executive Summary

Cassowary Tech Solutions is a domain training and project mentorship platform for undergraduate and postgraduate engineering students. We equip students with hands-on technical skills through structured training programs, then guide them through building their own major and minor projects — ensuring they deeply understand their work and can confidently present it at viva and evaluations.

🎯

2. Problem Statement

Engineering students across India struggle to bridge the gap between classroom theory and real-world technical implementation. Most lack access to domain-specific mentors, practical hands-on exposure to industry tools, and structured guidance for their major and minor projects. The result is projects built without genuine understanding — leaving students unable to explain or defend their own work. Cassowary Tech Solutions was founded to solve this: by training students in the domain first and mentoring them through every step of their project.

⚙️

3. Services Offered

🤖 AI & Machine Learning
Foundations, model training, NLP, computer vision — then guided project implementation
📊 Data Analytics
EDA, visualization, statistical modeling with Tableau & Power BI — live project walkthrough
🔐 Cybersecurity
Ethical hacking, network security, vulnerability assessment — simulation-based training
🌐 Web & App Development
Full-stack training from basics to deployment — mentor-guided project building
☁️ Cloud & DevOps
AWS/GCP/Azure, Docker, Kubernetes — hands-on training with real cloud project guidance
📝 Documentation & Research
IEEE format, SRS, synopsis, LaTeX — guided writing so you understand every section
🔬

4. Delivery Methodology

1
Enrolment & Domain Mapping — Student shares their domain of interest, project topic, university guidelines, and deadline. A mentor is assigned and a personalised learning plan is created.
2
Domain Training Sessions — The mentor conducts focused, hands-on training sessions covering core concepts, tools, frameworks, and industry practices relevant to the student's project domain.
3
Guided Project Building — The student builds the project themselves with the mentor guiding every step — architecture, code, testing, and iteration — via live sessions and WhatsApp support.
4
Review & Refinement — The mentor reviews the completed project for correctness, completeness, and alignment with university criteria, then guides the student through necessary refinements.
5
Submission Prep & Viva Support — Mentor helps finalise documentation, prepare the PPT, and practise viva questions — ensuring the student can confidently explain every aspect of their own project.
🔒

5. Quality & Confidentiality Policy

Every project delivered by Cassowary Tech Solutions is built from scratch and tailored specifically to the student's requirements. We maintain a strict confidentiality policy — student project details, personal information, and all shared materials are never disclosed to third parties. Projects are not reused or resold under any circumstances.
